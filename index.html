StoNet Research

StoNet-research (Storage/Network research) is a Computer Science research team at the VU Amsterdam. We at StoNet are a part of the Massivizing Computer Systems (AtLarge) team, a team that specializes in making computer systems scalable, reliable, performant, and efficient for everyone. The StoNet team in particular investigates how to massivize the network and storage aspects of computer systems. For more about us look at About, what we are currently working at Research Projects, and our publications at Publications.
